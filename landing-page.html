FAQS

What services do you offer?
  • Pick up services which include washing, ironing and folding.
  • In-house services which only includes washing.

Is there a minimum order?
  • Yes, there is a minimum order of 5kgs

Is there a pick-up and delivery charge?
  • No, this is already included in the total price.

When will my clothes be returned?
  • Once your clothes are ready, we will communicate with you on what time you want them delivered, for a period not longer than three days.

Is this service available every day?
  • Yes, we have laundry service providers who are open 7 days a week.
