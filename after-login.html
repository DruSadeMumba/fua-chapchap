Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get paid?

Clients pay us in cash or via Mpesa. We pay out to your MPESA account every week.

Q: How much do I pay Fua Chapchap?

We do not have any monthly fees, so you only pay for the clients we have offered you. We usually ask for 20% of the amount the customer has paid, depending on the weight of the clothes. You can get more information by contacting us.

Q: How long does it take to sign up?

A: Submitting your information takes just 5 minutes. After which we will ask questions about the type of laundry you offer i.e Mama Fua or Laundry mart

Q: What if I don't have a smartphone?

A: Our team suggest you a budget Android smartphone that you can buy or finance on installment basis 5% of your weekly earnings.

Q: How do I meet with the reffered clients

We will connect our customers to you. The client we offer you will be allocated in the same location with you so connecting will be made easy.
